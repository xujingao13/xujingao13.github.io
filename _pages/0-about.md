I am currently a **tenure-track Assistant Professor** at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (**[ECE](https://www.eee.hku.hk/)**), the University of Hong Kong (**[HKU](https://www.hku.hk/)**).
Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Computer Science Department of Carnegie Mellon University working with Prof. [Mahadev Satyanarayanan](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~satya/).
I received both my B.E. (2017) and Ph.D. (2022) degrees with highest honors at the School of Software, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. [Zheng Yang](http://tns.thss.tsinghua.edu.cn/~yangzheng/) and Prof. [Yunhao Liu](http://tns.thss.tsinghua.edu.cn/~yunhao/en.html).
